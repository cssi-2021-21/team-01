What is Petlify?

Petlify is an incredible app that allows you to meet pets up for adoption. Petlify is a place built on a world of possibility. The possibility of forming connections that could lead to more. If you’re here to meet new animals, find adoption centers, or just live in the now, you’ve come to the right place.

Petlify is simple and fun—use the Swipe Right feature to Like a pet, and if the owner Likes you back, it's a Match! Get ready to enter a world of endless possibilities.

We currently support dog and cat adoption.
