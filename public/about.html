What is Petlify?

Petlify is an app that allows you to meet pets up for adoption. We built Petlify with philosophy that every pet should have a kind and caring home. If you’re here to meet new animals, find adoption centers, or just look at cute animals, you’ve come to the right place.

Petlify is simple and fun—use the Swipe Right feature to Like a pet, and if the owner Likes you back, it's a Match! Get ready to meet the pet you've always wanted

We currently support dog and cat adoption.

Made by Freeman Jiang, Ricky Ho, and Ethan Kwon during Google CSSI 2021.
